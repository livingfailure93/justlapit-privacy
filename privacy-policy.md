# Privacy Policy for JustLapIt

Last updated: September 2025

## No Data Collection
JustLapIt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 

## What We Don't Do
- No analytics tracking
- No user accounts
- No internet connection required
- No advertisements
- No data sharing with third parties

## Local Storage Only
All settings (themes, sound preferences, timer configurations) are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

## Permissions
- POST_NOTIFICATIONS: For timer alerts when app is in background
- VIBRATE: For haptic feedback during timer transitions
